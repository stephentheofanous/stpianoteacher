FAQs

How long are your lessons?

I have found that 40 minutes is a good length for beginner lessons, and 60 minutes for more advanced students.

How much do you charge?

My first lesson is always free of charge. This introductory session is a great chance to work out what stage you are at and what your goals are.

After that, students book lessons in blocks of 6. This commitment benefits both the student and teacher.

40 minute lessons are £28 each and 60 minute lessons are £34.

What happens if I have to cancel a lesson?

For each block of 6, I will always try my best to reschedule the first cancellation providing at least 24 hours notice has been given.

For the second cancellation in a block, I will record, edit and send a 'make-up video lesson' providing at least 24 hours notice has been given.

Where do you teach?

As a travelling teacher, I offer the convenience of teaching in students' homes in Broxbourne, Hoddesdon, Ware and surrounding areas.
